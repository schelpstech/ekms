We are moulding pupils into leaders of the future

Founded in 2010, with a mandate to mould the future of learners through the provision of qualitative world -class education sustained through a culture of unequivocal education standards.

  • Our Vision
    • To build a vibrant and world class school, where children are given the solid education foundation they need, while inculcating in them high moral values and confidence that will make them outstanding.
  • Our Mission
    • To be in the frontline of the education sector, a pacesetter with unequivocal education standards that will produce well balanced children in every sphere of lives.

10 Achievements in the last 10 Years

21st Century Curriculum

Over the years, we have researched and developed an academic curriculum which are underpinned by an evidence based approach to education and child development.

A Competent and Committed Workforce

Over the years, we have committed to recruiting, training and keeping a workforce of happy and competent teachers.

Government Approved

Our quality of service over the years have measured up to attain recognition and approval from Government for the First School Leaving Certificate.

Awards & Trophies

Our Pupils have gone head to head in several state and national competitions and have been able to win trophies, this attest to the quality of our service.

Creative Arts Skill-up

The development of innate potentials and nurturing of talents has been our utmost priority and has been embeded into our curriculum.

Extra - Curricula Activities

We have developed non-academic educating programs that are strategic to the development of life skills and character moulding for our learners.

Serene Learning Environment

From the layout of our classrooms to the array of recreational gadgets in place and our safe and aesthetic structure, our pupils have always been willing to come into school.

ICT and STEM Facilities

We have over the years developed the capacity of our pupils in digital literacy, science, technology, arts and mathematics. STEM and ICT are core fundamentals of our curriculum at EKMS
